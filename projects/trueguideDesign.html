TrueGuide Mobile Design

Project Information

  • Category : UX Design
  • Project Date : Sep 2023 - Mar 2024
  • High Fidelity Prototype :  

TrueGuide is a mobile-based application specifically for Android users. This application is built to facilitate new agents in staying up-to-date with various activities conducted by TrueAgency. These activities include PSA Training, SM7, Academy, Product Certification, and others. The application also provides a notepad for agents who have lists of potential prospects, potential recruits, and other notes that can be recorded to support the needs of new agents.
