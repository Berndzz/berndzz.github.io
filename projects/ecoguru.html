The ITaFood App is a prototype application related to Italian cuisine. This application design was created to fulfill the final project of a UX Design class. Product Description: The ITa App is a food ordering application that offers various Italian dishes such as pizza, pasta, and others. The dishes are made with the best local Italian spices and seasonings that suit the taste of Indonesian people.
