Baby Grow

Project Information

  • Category : UX Design
  • Project Date : Mar 2024 - Apr 2024
  • High Fidelity Prototype :  

Baby Grow is an application specifically designed to support parents, especially mothers, in caring for their babies. The app combines learning, recording, and monitoring features, with a primary focus on ease of use and user-perceived benefits. Developed based on the TAM and UTAUT Methods, Baby Grow offers a simple and intuitive interface, making it easier for parents to monitor baby development such as weight and provide educational material. With features such as the Baby Report Form, Simulations for Babies, and Educational Videos, this application functions as a practical and informative tool, suitable for today's baby care needs.
